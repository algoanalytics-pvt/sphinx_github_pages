docker_debrief.get_dynamodb_data.sum_get_total_news(ticker_name, since, until, all_news)[source]

Get news for a given ticker from since to until.

Args:

ticker_name (str): Ticker name. since (datetime): Start date. until (datetime): End date. all_news (dict): Dictionary containing news for various tickers.

Returns:
tuple: A tuple containing the following elements:

  • news_count (list): List of dictionaries with date and news count.

  • news_sent (dict): Dictionary of news sentiment information.

  • today_news_count (int): Number of news for the specified end date.

  • today_news_sent (float): Sentiment score of news for the specified end date.
